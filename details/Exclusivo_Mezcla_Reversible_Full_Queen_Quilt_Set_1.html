Exclusivo Mezcla Reversible Full Queen Quilt Set

Transform your space with a quilt set that combines style and comfort. Crafted from ultra-soft microfiber fabric, it offers a luxurious feel while remaining lightweight and adaptable for all seasons.

This reversible quilt set features a modern diamond pattern on both sides, allowing you to switch up your decor effortlessly. The ultrasonic embossing adds an artistic, durable finish without the bulk of conventional quilting, making it a perfect blend of sophistication and practicality.

Ideal for bedrooms, guest rooms, or even small living spaces like an RV, it’s easy to care for with just a simple machine wash and tumble dry. Included in the set is a main piece measuring 90 inches wide x 96 inches long and two matching pillow shams (20 inches wide x 26 inches long), which can be used with or without a bedsheet.

Whether you're looking for a decorative bedspread or a cozy coverlet, this quilt set delivers both—offering warmth in winter and a cool, breathable feel in summer. It's designed to enhance your bedroom’s aesthetic without compromising on comfort or ease of maintenance.
